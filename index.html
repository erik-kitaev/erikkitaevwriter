STARBORN OF THE GALAXY

by Erik Kitaev

Mark Mylan was just a farm boy until Sentari hunters arrived and his hidden powers awakened—now he's the galaxy's only hope against an immortal villain who can corrupt anyone's mind. Armed with a Life Sword and the mysterious numen, Mark must stop the Empire before it devours the Milky Way, but victory might cost him everyone he loves.

FULL SYNOPSIS

Twenty-two years after the rise of the Vakryn Empire, the galaxy is in darkness. The Sentari—peace and balance guardians no longer—have been hunted to near extinction. Their temples lie in ashes, their knowledge destroyed. There are only a few remaining, in secret. On the peaceful farm world of Tormi, seventeen-year-old Mark Mylan believes the wars are a thing of the past—until he discovers that he can move things with his mind. His mentor, a crusty old farmer named Tom, knows the secret: he is a retired Sentari general, and Mark is one of the last born with the numen, the unusual energy once used to defend the galaxy. When Imperial Hunters visit the planet in search of survivors, Tom takes Mark to the ancient, long-abandoned Sentari temple on Zenithon, and Mark begins his training in the numen and how to wield a Life Sword—a sword that is powered by emotion itself. However, he is haunted by visions of a dark figure: the Lord of the Vakryn, the Emperor, who was once a Sentari before falling to darkness. As Mark's powers grow, tragedy strikes. His closest friend is killed in an Imperial ambush, and Mark is forced to confront the brutal reality of war, destiny, and the fine line between light and darkness. Powered by grief and vow, he sets out to rebuild the Sentari Order and bring down the Empire that destroyed everything he cares about. From star systems to ancient world ruins, amidst betrayal, war, and self-discovery, Mark's journey transforms from one of survival to one of revolution. In a cosmos ruled by fear, he must choose what kind of Starborn he would become—the savior of the light, or the next darkness to consume it.

READ THE FIRST CHAPTER

The galaxy hasn’t been the same since the Empire rose twenty-two years ago. The Sentari—once defenders of balance and wielders of mysterious energies—were almost wiped out. Most were hunted down and executed; the few left live in hiding. Just a year before, the galaxy thrived under a council of worlds and democratic cooperation. It was a time of peace and stability. Then the Vakryn arrived. The Vakryn were ancient beings who existed for thousands of years—they were the opposite of the Sentari. The conflict that followed, called the Great Empire War, lasted a single devastating year. When it ended, the Empire had risen, and the old order was shattered. The Sentari were declared traitors, their temples burned, their knowledge lost. Entire populations were enslaved. Since then, the Empire has swallowed nearby multiple star systems, spreading like a shadow across the stars. Now it threatens to consume the entire Milky Way. But this story isn’t about the rise of empires. It’s about one person. The only one who can truly tell what happened next. ____ Full Chapter 1 releasing soon on my social media.
